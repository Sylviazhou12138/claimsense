NARRATIVE DESCRIPTION
On the evening of October 19, 2024, a fire broke out in the kitchen of the insured
dwelling. The insured, Ms. Priya Nair-Chandrasekaran, had left the gas stovetop
unattended while cooking. A towel placed too close to the burner ignited, and the
flames spread to the wooden cabinetry above the stove before the insured was able
to respond.

The Sacramento Fire Department (Engine Company 14) arrived on scene at 18:34 and
controlled the fire within approximately 22 minutes. The fire was contained primarily
to the kitchen, though smoke and water damage extended to the adjacent dining room
and the hallway leading to the master bedroom.

No injuries were reported. The family vacated the premises and is currently staying
at a relative's home.
